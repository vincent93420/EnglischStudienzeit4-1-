About the Author

Nic Stone is an American writer. She writes books for young readers and teenagers.

Her books talk about racism, justice, and society. Dear Martin was published in 2018.

Summary

The story is about Justyce McAllister, a Black teenager. He is smart and wants to become a lawyer.

One night, Justyce has a very unfair experience with the police. After this, he starts writing letters to Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.

In the letters, he talks about racism, fear, and injustice. The book shows problems in American society.

Main Themes

  • Racism
  • Justice and injustice
  • Police violence
  • Identity
  • Friendship

Main Characters

  • Justyce McAllister: Main character, thoughtful and calm.
  • Quan Banks: His childhood friend.
  • Manny Rivers: A student with racist views.
  • Jared: Justyce’s best friend.

Important Quote

“I can’t change where I come from, but I can change where I’m going.”

This quote means that Justyce believes in change and a better future.
